Emacs自带的主题实在难看,决定换成以前常用的[[https://github.com/chriskempson/tomorrow-theme][ Tomorrow ]]主题系列.自己尝试了一下没更换成功,看样子Emacs自定义主题稍微有些复杂:)放狗搜了一下,找到一个可用的答案.原来需要先require Tomorrow一下,然后在使用color-theme-tomorrow.el中定义的主题名称就可以了.

#+begin_src
(add-to-list 'load-path "~/.emacs.d/color-theme/")
(require 'color-theme)
(load "~/.emacs.d/color-theme/color-theme-tomorrow.el")
(eval-after-load "color-theme"
  '(progn
      (color-theme-tomorrow-night)))
#+end_src

PS:需要color-theme,且我的Tomorrow放在~/.emacs.d/color-theme/目录之中.
