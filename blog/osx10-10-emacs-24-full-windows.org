Emacs 24.4 全屏化窗口的命令为toggle-frame-fullscreen, 为它绑定一个快捷键:

#+begin_src 
;; fullscreen
(global-set-key (kbd "C-x w f") 'toggle-frame-fullscreen)
#+end_src
